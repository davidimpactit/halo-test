This is Halo

There are 7 billion people, and growing, on our planet and we all need sources of good quality protein. It is estimated that we are sharing this space with 50 billion chickens reared annually for the production of meat or eggs. Not all of these chickens could possibly be all reared extensively in free-range systems, but we still have a duty to care for animals.

For the Egg Producer

Benefits of a Halo Mobile include low disease pressure with the building moved after each flock to fresh pasture.

For the Meat Producer

The demand for quality table birds is growing. Halo Mobile steel framed houses provide the absolute best welfare friendly accommodation.

For the Specialist Rearer

Halo Mini Ranger & Discovery are fully insulated, can be sub-divided, are moveable, can accommodate detachable verandas and will lower disease pressure.

Our Range

Halo Mini Discovery

Designed to look like a more traditional Poultry Ark, the Mini Discovery is our most popular building for the small producers who are supplying an egg round, farm shop or marketing premium table meat. The apex design provides a really high quality finish to the building.

One important advantage of the Apex design is the superior roof ventilation system which provides the best possible system for bringing a continuous draw of fresh air from the side vents through the roof vent. The Halo Mini Discovery is designed for smaller flock sizes and can be used for layers, broilers and very often as a specialist rearing unit for laying or game birds

Halo Mini Ranger

The Halo Mini Ranger is designed for smaller flock sizes and can be used for layers, broilers and very often as a specialist rearing unit for laying or game birds. The mobile is 5m wide and built in modular 2.5m section. For laying birds, the design can incorporate options for slatted sections for the placing of nest boxes and feeders, together with a dedicated indoor scratch area.

The Halo Mini Ranger can also be built with a dedicated egg room or store room and comes complete with full side venting, gutters to ensure the immediate area around the mobile stays dry and indoor or outdoor frames for the placing of feed bins and water tanks.

Halo Ranger

The mobile is built in modular 2.5msections and incorporates options for slated sections for the placing of nest boxes and feeders, together with a dedicated indoor scratch area with ready access through pop holes to an outdoor area where the birds can roam freely.

The Halo Ranger can also be built with a specialist egg room and comes complete with full side venting (manual or electronic options available), gutters to ensure the immediate area around the mobile stays dry and indoor or outdoor frames for the placing of feed bins and water tanks.
